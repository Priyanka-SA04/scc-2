Welcome to the Resource Management System

Efficiently manage and track resources across vendors, hospitals, and deliveries. The SmartCareConnect website is a comprehensive resource management system designed to streamline interactions between hospitals and vendors, ensuring efficient handling of medical supplies.

Hospital

Provides healthcare facilities with a centralized platform to manage procurement and inventory needs. Hospitals can effortlessly view and update their profiles, track both current and past orders, and place new orders with specified urgency levels...

Vendor

SmartCareConnect's vendor dashboard empowers suppliers with tools to very efficiently manage and fulfill hospital orders. Vendors can categorize pending orders based on urgency and nearest proximity, greatly optimizing delivery schedules and tasks.

Delivery

Enhances logistics coordination by providing delivery personnel with real-time updates.This interface supports efficient communication between vendors and hospitals, facilitating smooth operations and ensuring healthcare facilities receive essential supplies promptly.
